Welcome to my web page! I'm currently a student at Epicodus (Seattle Campus), on track to graduate with a Certificate in Web and Mobile Development in November 2019.

My background is in geology. As a student in geology, I romped around landscapes all over the United States mapping rock outcrops, studying soil profiles, and researching active faults. As a professional, I spent the last 2.5 years assessing and mitigating geologic hazards such as landslides, soil settlement, and liquefaction in Washington and Oregon.

While working as a geologist, I thought creating a mobile app someday would be fun. So I started teaching myself JavaScript on Codecademy in my free time. This new hobby reminded me of how much fun I had learning and using MATLAB for my graduate research. After I learned about coding boot camps and spoke to others who had completed them, I decided it was time for me to pursue a new career in tech.
